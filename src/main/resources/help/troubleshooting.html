Troubleshooting

This page covers common issues and their solutions. If your problem is not listed here, check the launcher logs for error details.

Download Failures

Files fail to download or verification errors appear during launch.

  • Check your internet connection and try again.
  • If you are on a corporate or school network, configure a proxy in Settings under Network / Proxy.
  • Retry the launch — transient network errors often resolve on a second attempt.
  • If a specific file consistently fails, its download URL may be broken. Report this to the modpack author.

Game Crashes

The game starts but crashes shortly after, or crashes during gameplay.

  • Enable the Game Console in settings to view crash output in real time.
  • Check the crash report in the modpack's crash-reports/ folder for specific error details.
  • Try increasing the maximum RAM allocation in Settings — many modpacks need at least 4 - 6 GB.
  • Right-click the modpack on the main screen and select Open Modpack Folder, then delete the mods/ folder and relaunch to force a fresh download.
Warning

Allocating too much RAM (more than your system can spare) can cause crashes just as easily as allocating too little. Keep maximum RAM at or below half your total system memory.

Launcher Stuck on Loading

The launcher appears frozen or a progress bar stops moving.

  • Wait a few minutes — large modpacks can take time to verify files, especially on first launch.
  • Check the launcher logs for errors (enable Enhanced Logging in settings).
  • As a last resort, use Reset Launcher in Settings to return to a clean state.

Offline Mode

The launcher requires an internet connection for authentication and downloading files. However, modpack manifests and game files are cached locally after the first successful download.

  • If you lose connectivity after initial setup, the game may still launch using cached files.
  • Authentication tokens are cached for approximately 4 hours — if your token is still valid, you can play offline within that window.

Performance Issues

The game runs but with low FPS or frequent stuttering.

  • Switch to the Performance (Aikar's Flags) JVM preset in Settings.
  • Ensure you have allocated enough RAM — at least 4 GB for most modpacks.
  • Close other memory-intensive applications while playing.
  • Check that your graphics drivers are up to date.

Missing Mods or Files

The game launches but mods are missing or configs are wrong.

  • The launcher verifies file hashes on each launch. If files were manually modified, they will be re-downloaded to match the modpack definition.
  • If a mod is missing from the modpack itself, contact the modpack author.
  • Try removing the modpack and re-adding it to force a complete re-download.

Authentication Issues

Cannot sign in or keep getting prompted to re-authenticate.

  • Tokens expire after approximately 4 hours — re-authentication is normal.
  • If sign-in fails repeatedly, try clearing your browser cookies for login.microsoftonline.com.
  • Ensure your Microsoft account has an active Minecraft license.
  • See Login for more details on the authentication process.
Tip

When reporting issues, always include: the modpack name and version, your operating system, the launcher log file, and any crash reports. This helps diagnose problems much faster.
